## How to Get Coordinates

### Method 1: Google Maps
1. Open [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com)
2. Search for "Almeria, Biliran"
3. Right-click on your specific location
4. Click on the coordinates to copy them
5. Format: First number is latitude, second is longitude

### Method 2: OpenStreetMap
1. Visit [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org)
2. Search for "Almeria, Biliran, Philippines"
3. Right-click on your location
4. Select "Show address"
5. Coordinates will be displayed

### Method 3: GPS Device/Phone
1. Use your phone's GPS or Maps app
2. Drop a pin at your location
3. View coordinates in the location details
4. Ensure they're in decimal format (not degrees/minutes/seconds)

## Coordinate Format
Always use **decimal degrees** format:
- ✅ Correct: `11.6447, 124.3931`
- ❌ Wrong: `11°38'41"N, 124°23'35"E`

## Tips for Accurate Coordinates

1. **Zoom in** on the map before selecting a location
2. **Verify** coordinates are within Almeria bounds
3. **Test** by entering them in the map to see if they appear in the right location
4. **Use at least 4 decimal places** for accuracy (e.g., 11.6447)

## Troubleshooting

### Coordinates Not Showing on Map
- Verify they're within the valid range
- Check format (decimal degrees, not DMS)
- Ensure latitude comes before longitude

### Map Won't Pan to Location
- Coordinates might be outside Almeria bounds
- Map is restricted to Almeria area only
- Use coordinates within specified ranges

### Need More Precise Locations?
- Use Google Maps Street View for exact addresses
- Zoom in to building level before getting coordinates
- Cross-reference with local landmarks
