User Guide

Learn how to use Waste Care step by step

1

Create Your Account

Start by creating your Waste Care account to access all features.

  1. Click the "Register" button on the homepage
  2. Fill in your username, email, and password
  3. Select your role (Customer for regular users)
  4. Click "Create Account" to complete registration
Tip: Use a valid email address to receive appointment confirmations and updates.
2

Login to Your Account

Access your dashboard by logging in.

  1. Click the "Login" button
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. Click "Sign In"
  4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard
3

Schedule a Waste Pickup

Request a waste collection service in just a few clicks.

  1. Go to Dashboard and click "Schedule Pickup"
  2. Select your Waste Type (General, Recyclable, Organic, etc.)
  3. Enter your complete Pickup Address
  4. Choose your preferred Pickup Day (Monday-Sunday)
  5. Click on the map to set your exact location
  6. Select your Preferred Date and Time
  7. Set Priority Level and estimate the waste weight
  8. Add any special instructions or notes
  9. Click "Submit Request"
Success: You'll receive a confirmation email and notification once your request is submitted.
4

Track Your Appointments

Monitor the status of your pickup requests.

  1. Navigate to Appointments → All Appointments
  2. View all your pickup requests with their current status
  3. Click on any appointment to see full details
  4. Use filters to search by status or date
Requested: Waiting for approval
Scheduled: Approved and scheduled
In Progress: Team is on the way
Completed: Pickup finished
5

Use Calendar View

Visualize your appointments on a calendar.

  1. Go to Appointments → Calendar View
  2. See all your pickups organized by date
  3. Click on any date to view appointments
  4. Navigate between months using arrow buttons
6

Stay Updated with Notifications

Receive real-time updates about your appointments.

  1. Click the Notifications icon in the navbar
  2. View all your notifications and updates
  3. Get notified when your appointment status changes
  4. Mark notifications as read after viewing
7

Review Service History

Access your past completed pickups.

  1. Navigate to History in the menu
  2. View all your completed waste pickups
  3. Check dates, weights, and service details
  4. Track your environmental impact over time
8

Manage Your Profile

Update your account information and settings.

  1. Click your username in the top right corner
  2. Select "Profile" to view your information
  3. Click "Settings" to update your details
  4. Change your name, email, or other preferences
  5. Click "Save Changes" when done

Helpful Tips

Be Specific: Provide detailed addresses and special instructions for smooth pickups.
Plan Ahead: Schedule pickups at least 24 hours in advance for better service.
Separate Waste: Sort your waste by type for easier recycling and processing.
Check Notifications: Stay updated on your appointment status through notifications.
Need More Help?

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team.
